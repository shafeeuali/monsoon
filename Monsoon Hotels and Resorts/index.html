About us

Monsoon Hotels & Resorts is a privately held company active in hospitality property development and stands for quality and excellence in its delivery.

We have the capacity and experience to develop hotels and resorts that strictly adhere to the standards and market demands. We offer great investment opportunities with a fairly short investment return period to potential investors by developing turnkey income producing hotels and resorts and offering them for operations and management by globally well-known hospitality brands.

To strengthen profitability, we target major international hospitality brands and utilize their customer base as our launch pad.

Hospitality industry hotels and resorts means more than developing just a property. In the process we make sure that we deliver and preserve the legacy, the touch, and the expectations of the brand for which we develop the property. While on a much higher level the property development is only the tip of the iceberg. The development stages includes strategic analysis, development and management auditing, and continuous market surveillance.

The result of this approach, is that we also succeed to meet the investor's expectations by creating a stable income producing asset.

Consistently achieving goals every time is a difficult track record to maintain but its not impossible by following a sophisticated roadmap.
